Our Inspiration

This game was inspired by the classic cookie clicker game. We thought it would be a really cool idea to create a game that enables the user to create donuts rather than cookies and increase the amount of cookies created at once. It was a lot of fun to create and very cool to see in action! You can take a look at the original cookie clicker game here: Cookie Clicker Game
